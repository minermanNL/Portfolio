About Me

From a young age, I have been deeply fascinated by technology and music. This dual interest has significantly influenced my personal and professional development.

My journey began with building custom PCs for friends and family, which honed my technical skills and led to freelance opportunities in IT support and system integration. Simultaneously, I explored music production, experimenting with various genres and eventually establishing myself in the Phonk, Trap, and House music scenes.

Balancing my IT career with music production has been both challenging and rewarding. This balance has allowed me to develop a diverse skill set and adapt to various creative and technical demands.

I am committed to continuous learning, regularly updating my knowledge in emerging technologies and music trends. Engaging with online communities and attending industry events has enriched my understanding and network in both fields.
